Growmore General Trading, Kuwait


After the Kuwait invasion in 1991, the promoters of Al-Zahem & Malhotra saw the opportunity for security services in Kuwait and entered into a joint venture with the international chain of Security providers GROUP 4 Securites.

The company specializes in the installation of security systems and guarding services. To its credit, the company has the accounts of the 7 out of the 9 banks in Kuwait and is today a leading player in the field of security services.

In 2001, the company has achieved a turnover of US$3.6 million.
