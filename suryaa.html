Crowne Plaza Surya, New Delhi, India


In the heart of New Friends Colony, New Delhi stands one of the most prestigious addresses, the Suryaa, a five-star deluxe hotel built in 1982 offering luxuriously appointed 230 guest rooms and suites, providing elegance and comfort to make the stay truly memorable.

The multi-cuisine restaurants and bar feature varied culinary experiences providing a wide selection of food and beverage facilities, and offer all-day dining, plus 24-hour Room Service and a discotheque. Conference and banqueting facilities are suitable for any occasion, with the additional services of a Business Centre. Extensive fitness facilities include an open-air swimming pool and a fully equipped gymnasium for guests' relaxation and exercise.

This hotel, which stands for true excellence in courtesy and hospitality, is also conveniently located close to Delhi's prime commercial center and is an ideal base from which to explore the tourist and historical attractions of the city.
